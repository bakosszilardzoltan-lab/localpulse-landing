FAQ

Questions? Answered.

How quickly will I see results? +
Most customers see their first ranking improvements within 7–14 days. Significant gains typically happen within the first 30–60 days as our AI implements optimizations and builds citation authority.
Do I need technical knowledge? +
Not at all. LocalPulse is built for business owners and marketers, not developers. Setup takes under 10 minutes and our AI handles the technical SEO automatically.
Can I manage multiple locations? +
Yes! Our Growth plan supports up to 5 locations and our Agency plan supports unlimited locations with client management features, white-label reports, and team access.
Is there a free trial? +
Yes, all plans include a 14-day free trial. No credit card required. You get full access to all features for your chosen plan during the trial period.
What if I want to cancel? +
Cancel anytime with no penalties or fees. Your account stays active until the end of your billing period. We believe in earning your business every month.
Does this work for any type of local business? +
LocalPulse works for any business with a physical location or service area. Restaurants, medical practices, law firms, home services, retail stores — if local customers search for you, we can help you rank higher.
